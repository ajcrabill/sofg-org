Join A Cohort

The next SOFG Cohorts begin in February. To complete the application, click here

Here are the 2025 Cohorts available through the Council of the Great City Schools. The three Cohorts are: IMPLEMENTING SOFG BOARD, IMPLEMENTING SOFG STAFF, and ADVANCED GOVERNANCE & COACHING. They are offered as an opportunity to guide school boards toward a student outcomes focused approach to governance. Please read thoroughly to ensure understanding and check against your calendar for known commitments before applying. Please apply ONLY IF you are willing and able to commit to the dates and times indicated below. If you have questions before applying, please reach out to ajcrabill@cgcs.org or celsbernd@cgcs.org for clarification.

The application will be open through 12/1/2024. Priority will be given to applications received by 11/1/2024
